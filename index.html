Frequently Asked Questions

How do I fly from Stockholm to Coimbatore?

There are no direct flights from Europe to Coimbatore. You'll need to fly via a major Indian city such as Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, or Bangalore.

Recommended approach:

  • Book a flexible international ticket from Stockholm to one of these major Indian cities
  • Popular airlines: Emirates, Qatar Airways, Lufthansa
  • Book your domestic flight to Coimbatore separately (see below)

Important: We advise against booking a "direct" Stockholm‚ÄìCoimbatore ticket through a single booking system, as this may limit flexibility.

What about connecting terminals in India?

International and domestic terminals are separate at Indian airports:

  • Delhi, Chennai, Bangalore: ~1‚Äì2 km apart
  • Mumbai: ~4‚Äì5 km apart

Note: You'll need to collect your baggage at the international arrival point and re-check it for your domestic flight.

How do I book domestic flights in India?

Booking domestic flights in India is easy and flexible:

  • You can book a few months after your international ticket
  • Use Indian airline websites or Swedish sites like Skyscanner.se or Momondo.se
  • IndiGo is the most popular and reliable domestic airline

Note: We are here to help with booking. Please reach out to us.

What about baggage allowance in Domestic flights in India?

  • Default baggage allowance: 15 kg
  • Extra baggage can be added easily and is very affordable
